Irish Cream Coffee Creamer

Description

This Irish cream coffee creamer, velvety and elegant, elevates your morning cup of Joe.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 4 tsp instant espresso
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 cup half and half

Directions

  1. Heat heavy cream and sugar together in a small saucepan over medium heat, stirring occasionally until sugar dissolves. Whisk in cocoa powder, espresso, vanilla, almond extract and salt. Remove from heat and cool slightly. Stir in half-and-half and refrigerate until ready to serve.
